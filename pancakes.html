Pancakes

Nigerian Pancakes are a delightful variation of the traditional pancake, known for their rich, slightly sweet, and savory flavor. Unlike the typical American pancake, Nigerian pancakes are made with a batter that includes ingredients like sugar, onions, and sometimes peppers, giving them a unique taste. The pancakes are cooked on a griddle or frying pan until golden brown, with a soft and slightly crispy texture. They are often enjoyed as a breakfast dish or snack, served plain or with toppings like honey, syrup, or fruit. Nigerian Pancakes are loved for their versatility and the blend of flavors that make them both satisfying and delicious.

prep time

10 mins.

cook time

15 mins.

serving

22 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Appetizers

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, add the flour, sugar, baking, powder, nutmeg, and salt. Mix well.

step 2

In a separate bowl, add the eggs, milk, and melted butter together. Whisk well

step 3

Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until the mixture becomes smooth and lump free.

step 4

Heat a griddle or pan and add about 1 to 2 teaspoons of oil.

step 5

Ladle about 1/4 of a cup (more or less depending on how big you want your pancakes to be) at a time into the Pan.

step 6

Spread the batter into a circle by rotating and tilting the Pan (you can check video above)

step 7

Flip to the other side as soon as you see the batter is beginning to solidify

step 8

Cook the other side for about 30-40 seconds or till light Brown

step 9

This yielded 10 medium sized Pancakes.

ingredients

2 cups all-purpose Flour

1/4 cup sugar 4 Tablespoons

1 teaspoon baking Powder

1/4 teaspoon nutmeg or cinnamon

1 pinch salt

2 cups milk

2 eggs

3 tablespoons melted butter

oil For cooking

tools

Grill or Charcoal Grill

Skewers

Mixing Bowl

Knife and Cutting Board

Basting Brush

Measuring Spoons

Grill Tongs
