Fried Indomie & Eggs

Nigerian Fried Indomie and Eggs is a popular, quick, and delicious dish that combines instant noodles with scrambled eggs for a satisfying meal. This easy-to-make recipe involves frying the Indomie noodles with spices and seasonings, then mixing in scrambled eggs for added richness and flavor. The dish is versatile and can be customized with additional ingredients like vegetables, peppers, or meats. prep time

5 mins.

cook time

10 mins.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Appetizers

instructions

step 1

Heat up a pot with a sachet of pure water, once its boiling hot, add 1 sachet of noodles spice and allow it cook for a minute before adding your noodles.

step 2

Once your noodles is almost tender and the water is dry, remove from heat and set aside. Set another pot or frying pan on heat and once it's hot, add vegetable oil, once your veg oil is hot enough, add your onion and stir.

step 3

After a minute, add your pepper, remaining noodles spice, green bell pepper and carrot, and stir fry for another 3 to 4 mins.

step 4

Pour in your already cooked noodles into your veggies stir and mix everything together. Stir and allow it cook for another 3 to 4 minutes, then remove from heat.

step 5

To fry your plain eggs: Add your vegetable oil into a small frying pan, once your oil is hot, gently break your eggs into a bowl with the yolk and egg white still in tack, gently pour into your frying pan and flip over after a minute.

step 6

Turn off heat, once you flip over; the heat from the pan will continue the cooking process. Serve and enjoy your meal.
